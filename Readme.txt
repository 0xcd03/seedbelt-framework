Seedbelt Framework is an HTML UI Kit inspired by the look and behavior of chatbots.
It's a fast and effective way to validate your startup or business idea before you double down on it. Build an effective landing page with Seedbelt, test your idea, scale. 

Learn More: http://seedbelt-framework.com/

---

Folder structure:
_examples — Useful example to get you started quickly.
_faq — Frequently asked questions. Mini version of a manual.
_templates — Legal pages / FAQ templates
start-template.html — Empty template. Start there.
library.html — Library of the blocks.
